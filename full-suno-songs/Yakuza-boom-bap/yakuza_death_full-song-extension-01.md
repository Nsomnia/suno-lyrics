**Style and sound description:**
```
This experimental NYC east coast Boom-Bap track opens with retro reel-to-reel martial arts vocal samples and city sfx, thunderous Tam-tam gong, then explodes into warped apocalyptic breakbeats, layered syncopated manipulated mechanical grinding synths, and forceful wobble dub bass. Verse drive builds with feudal Japanese and classic Chinese timbres (Shimano Shashimi, wooden blocks, tam-tam, Asian strings) against gritty rap and unpredictable percussion. High-contrast goofy monk interludes cut through Xao water flutes. Hook raw call-and-responsible gang chants escalate into dynamic, chord-shifting, modal and vocalist gender changing, choruses with explosive, raw soaring vocals. Dense sampling, ghost notes, off-beat MCP3000, and THX & Dolby Atmos studio mastering on a top quality SACD, create an immersive, frenetic sound world, that grows and shows off the Suno model.
```
